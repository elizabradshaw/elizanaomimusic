About

I am an indie-pop singer/songwriter and multi-instrumentalist based in Logan, Utah. I have received a degree from Utah State University, and have been teaching cello lessons since 2014. As a Teaching Artist, I am deeply invested in the arts and education, and my goal is to cultivate a deep appreciation for music. As a continuous learner, I've attended over a dozen music conferences and masterclasses, and have completed a music teaching training program at Songsmith Studio. Whether you're a beginner or ready to take your skills to the next level, I am passionate about teaching and providing a rich learning experience.

Lessons With Me

All my knowledge is available to my students I have taught cello lessons for almost a decade. I also teach songwriting lessons. Many of my songwriting students benefit from my skills ukulele, singing, guitar, piano, music theory, and studio recording. Many of my students end up studying several of these topics simultaneously, and are able to utilize recording equipment.
I'm experienced with kids and teenagers. I taught over 200 group music classes and developed comprehensive curriculum as a music specialist at camp chenawanda. I also was an assistant teacher for kids at Mountain West String Academy. My lessons are engaging and are customized to the needs of each student
Location is flexible. Whether you prefer the convenience of online lessons, the comfort of your own home, or the focused environment of my home studio, I am happy to accommodate your needs. I have utilized over 5 online teaching platforms, such as TakeLessons, to make music accessible to everyone.

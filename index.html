Eliza Naomi

About

I'm an indie-pop singer, songwriter, and multi-instrumentalist based in Eugene, Oregon. I received a degree from Utah State University and have been teaching lessons since I was a high schooler in 2014. I teach singing/songwriting, cello, ukulele, piano, and guitar lessons. Many of my students end up studying several of these topics simultaneously.

I create a space where authenticity shines over polished perfection. Music is the canvas of our inner world, painted into sound. It's a way to empower ourselves and discover who we are, I invite you to join me in pushing past limits, making music that's as diverse and vibrant as the human experience itself.

About

I am an indie-pop singer/songwriter, multi-instrumentalist, and cello teacher based in Logan, Utah. I am passionate about teaching lessons, performing in ensembles, and writing original music. Growing up, my home was always filled with music. Being one of 11 children, I am no stranger to the chaos of life, and I find music in the midst of it all. I tell my story through heartfelt lyrics and communicate my emotions through the cello, songwriting, ukulele, piano, and guitar. As a Teaching Artist, I am not just a musician who gives lessons to pay the bills while waiting for a big break. My work is deeply rooted in both the arts and education, and I am passionate about sharing my knowledge and experience with others. I believe that music is not just about learning an instrument or playing a song, but about developing a deep appreciation for the art form and connecting with oneself and others. That's why I strive to create a meaningful and enriching experience for my students, whether they are just starting out or looking to take their skills to the next level.

Lessons With Me

All my knowledge is available to my students. I primarily teach cello and songwriting, but I also have experience teaching ukulele, singing, beginner guitar, and beginner piano. Many of my students end up studying several of these topics simultaneously.
I'm experienced with kids. With a wealth of experience teaching children aged 6-17, I am comfortable leading group music classes in piano, songwriting, and basic music theory. Additionally, I am equally capable of working with adults.
Location is flexible. Whether you prefer the convenience of online lessons, the comfort of your own home, or the focused environment of my home studio, I am happy to accommodate your needs.

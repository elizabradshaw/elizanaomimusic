Eliza Naomi

About

I'm an indie-pop singer, songwriter, and multi-instrumentalist based in Eugene, Oregon. I received a degree from Utah State University and have been teaching lessons since I was a high schooler in 2014. I teach singing/songwriting, cello, ukulele, guitar, and keyboard lessons. Many of my students end up studying several of these topics simultaneously.

I create a space where authenticity shines over polished perfection. Music is the canvas of our inner world, painted into sound. It's a way to empower ourselves and discover who we are, I invite you to join me in pushing past limits, making music that's as diverse and vibrant as the human experience itself.

My Studio

All of my knowledge is available to my students I teach cello, singing/songwriting, ukulele, guitar, and keyboard lessons. Many of my students end up studying several of these topics simultaneously.
I’m experienced with all ages But I currently focus on students ages 8 and older, especially teens and adults. I've taught over 200 group music classes and countless private lessons, customizing each lesson to suit individual learning needs.
Location is flexible. Whether you prefer the convenience of online lessons, or the focused environment of my home studio in Eugene Oregon, I am happy to accommodate your needs.

Recitals

Hello everyone! Whether you're a current student, a former student, or simply a friend or family member, I’m excited to invite you to Eliza Naomi’s Creative Circle – an open mic that celebrates all forms of creativity! Held on Zoom, this event is a unique opportunity to share whatever you’ve been working on, whether it’s a new song, a poem, a piece of visual art, or a story. The Creative Circle is all about self-expression in a relaxed, supportive setting. It’s free to attend, and performers have a chance to earn tips from the audience as a token of appreciation. This is a space where everyone can feel comfortable sharing their authentic selves, without the pressure of perfection.
